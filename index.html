The Artist

Cortez Tattoos

A hand guided by heritage

Raised on the murals, lowriders, and hand-painted signs of East Los Angeles, Cortez turned a childhood spent sketching into a lifetime devoted to the needle. Over fifteen years he's refined a single-needle technique that turns smoke, shadow, and devotion into skin-deep stories.

Every piece begins as a conversation. Cortez listens for the people, the prayers, and the places that made you — then renders them in the soft gradients and razor-sharp script that define authentic Chicano black & grey.

  • Single-needle fine line
  • Black & grey realism
  • Old English & script lettering
  • Religious & cultural iconography
